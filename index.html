Hi. We're Young Government Leaders in Chicago.

An official chapter of the national professional organization for government employees. We are building an engaged community of public servants in Chicago.

Learn about what we do

Here's all the stuff we do.

We are a civic community of public servants.
Providing providing professional, service, and social opportunities to over 500 local, state, and federal govvies in Chicago.

Connect

YGL Chicago fosters a network of public servants and facilitates collaboration, serving shared interests. Go beyond the office through outreach events and service projects.

Grow

YGL Chicago promotes growth, hosting career development programs and events. Expect to strengthen and expand leadership competencies in a supportive environment.

Inspire

Spark innovation by drawing on experience of public servants, community members, and established leaders, YGL Chicago is a shared stage where government leaders unite.
